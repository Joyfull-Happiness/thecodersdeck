✨Mental Break MagicMagic✨

Mental Break Magic is a digital wellness tool designed specifically for coders and problem-solvers. Built with love, creativity, and a touch of Black Girl Magic, this page offers quick, intentional mental breaks to help you reset when the bugs won’t budge and the logic loops won’t end. From mindful movement and power poses to joyful affirmations and playful prompts, each activity is crafted to help you step away from the screen, recharge your brain, and return with fresh eyes and renewed energy. Whether you need a dance break, a deep breath, or just a reminder that you’re the queen/king/legend of code—this space is for you.
